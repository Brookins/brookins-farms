Grain Operation

Currently expanded to almost 200 acres. Crops rotated between Soybeans and Corn

Last year for harvest averaged around 220 bushels/arre for Corn

Soybean harvest also came in higher than normal at 65 bushels/acre

Poultry Operation

The farm has three poultry barns (seen in title picture)

The barns are 60'x 600'and are able to hold around 45,000 chickens at one time

Last year a total of seven groups of chickens were raised in each barn
